About Us

Welcome to our website! Here, we celebrate Slovak language, culture, and heritage. Learn more about who we are and what we do.

Our Mission

Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester is a registered charity dedicated to preserving and promoting the Slovak language and culture. We provide educational resources and organize cultural events to help Slovak-speaking families maintain their heritage while living abroad.

Our Vision

We aim to build a community that bridges the gap between the UK and Slovakia, supporting families through education and cultural activities that promote the Slovak language and traditions.
